Getting There

By rail: Barnes station is a 12-min walk (South Western Railway from Waterloo).

By tube: Putney Bridge station (District Line) is a 20-min walk or short taxi ride.

By bus: Routes 33, 337 stop nearby.

By car: Limited parking on site (we recommend Asda Putney car park, 5 min walk).

Pre-book taxis home!
📷

Dress Code

No strict dress code here, we just want everyone feeling good and having fun! Think colour, texture, and summer vibes. Linen suits, flowy dresses, bold prints (whatever makes you feel great). Wear what you're comfortable in and come ready to celebrate.

The only request: please avoid white and sage green, which are reserved for the bridal party.

A note about the ceremony

We have chosen to keep our ceremony small and intimate, and if you're joining us as a day or evening guest, we hope you'll understand that this is in no way a reflection of how much you mean to us. The truth is, we have so many wonderful people in our lives that we wanted to make sure that when we say our vows, we are fully present in that moment (just the two of us, taking it all in, without feeling the weight of too many eyes on us).

We are so grateful to have every single one of you as part of our big day, the celebration would not be the same without you, and we cannot wait to share it with you.

With all our love, Maike & Tommy ♥
